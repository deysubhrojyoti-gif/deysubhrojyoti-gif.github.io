About

Co-Founder & COO · Toothsure®

I am a product manager and entrepreneur who spent five years inside Hasbro's global product operations, delivering NERF, Fortnite, and pre-school lines across North America, Europe, and Asia, before co-founding a regulated medical device company, navigating the FDA, and building two supply chains simultaneously on two continents.

My engineering foundation spans embedded systems firmware, PCB design, electromechanical product development, VR simulation architecture, and production AI/LLM deployment. I have taken products from whiteboard through FDA clearance, from concept through global manufacturing scale-up.

I hold an MS in Computer Engineering from the University of Michigan-Dearborn (GPA 3.64) and a BS in Electrical Engineering from KIIT University. Based in Dearborn, Michigan.
